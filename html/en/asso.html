Framasoft association

Framasoft network relies on a large community of volunteer contributors but could not exist without the support of an association.

Framasoft is an association constituted in accordance with the French law of 1901 concerning non-profit organizations 1901. As it is labelled of public interest, tax deductibility is authorized by the French law for donations. We have currently 3 permanent employees and about 20 active members.
